Man is the only
creature
who refuses
to be what he is.
I have realized
that the
past and future
are real illusions,
that they
exist in the present,
which is what
there is and all
there is.
“Two things are infinite:
the universe
and human stupidity;
and I'm not sure
about the universe.”

― Albert Einstein
I am interested only in
"nonsense";
only in that which
makes no practical sense.
I am interested in
life only in its absurd
manifestations.
All problems
are
illusions of
the mind.
